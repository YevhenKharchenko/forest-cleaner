About the Game

A Simple Tap Can Save the Earth!
Forest Cleaner is a peaceful, eco-conscious game where your job is to clean up polluted forests by collecting litter and reviving nature. Every action brings visible change—from trash to treasure, from gray to green. Relax, clean, and rebuild the environment one tap at a time.

Features

  • Interactive Cleanup:

    Tap to pick up trash and watch the environment transform.

  • Beautiful Forests:

    Each area becomes more lush and vibrant as you clean.

  • Satisfying Progress:

    Visually rewarding as you return the forest to life.

  • Eco Message:

    A casual way to promote environmental awareness.

  • Soothing Sounds:

    Calming background music and nature effects.

Benefits

  • Safe for All Ages:

    Educational and non-violent fun for kids and adults.

  • Stress-Free Gameplay:

    Designed to relax you while you play.

  • Positive Vibes:

    Every task feels meaningful and constructive.

  • Offline Mode:

    Play anytime, anywhere—no connection needed.
